Customizing Your Invoice Template

All InvoiceCompass templates can be fully customized to match your brand and business needs. Here's how to make the most of our templates:

  1. Add your logo and branding: Upload your company logo and customize colors to match your brand.
  2. Customize fields: Add, remove, or modify fields to suit your specific business requirements.
  3. Set default values: Save time by setting default values for fields you use frequently.
  4. Add payment terms: Clearly communicate your payment expectations and accepted methods.
  5. Include notes: Add custom notes or terms and conditions to provide additional information.
